Colony Collapse Disorder

This disease has been around for a long time. It has only been a problem as the industrialization of farming and the use of toxic chemicals became the dominate agricultural practice.

Primary causes are

  • Chemical contamination.

    Eg Neo nicitoids, Fungicides, glycosides

  • bio diversity

    . Or lack of
  • Internal Hive Management.
  • Excessive movement of bees.
